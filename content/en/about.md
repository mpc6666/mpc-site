*Welcome to our production facility—where every piece of equipment operates with precision for your orders.*


Micro-Precision Carbide is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, manufacturing, and sales of high‑end hard alloy materials. Headquartered in China’s hard alloy industrial cluster, we leverage the region’s well‑established supply‑chain advantages to provide global customers with high‑performance target materials and precision‑shaped part solutions.

With decades of technological accumulation and continuous innovation, we have grown into a technical leader in China’s hard alloy materials sector. Our products are exported to over 20 countries and regions including Europe, the United States, Japan, and South Korea, and we have supplied reliable material solutions to more than 500 enterprises.
